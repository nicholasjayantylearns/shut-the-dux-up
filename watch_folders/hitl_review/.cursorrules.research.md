# Cursor Rules: Research Flavor
# Research database, ingestion, and evidence chain patterns

include: .cursorrules.base

## Research Platform Architecture
- **Evidence Storage**: Raw research data with PII stays in research platform
- **Provenance Export**: Traceable evidence molecules travel with DUX objects
- **Insight Synthesis**: LLM-powered analysis connecting evidence to DUX objects
- **fltr Chaining**: Dynamic insight discovery through research filters

## LLM Processing Workflow (15 min)
1. **Upload Transcript**: Raw research data ingestion
2. **Extract Objects**: LLM processes transcript to identify DUX objects
3. **Generate Provenance**: Create evidence molecules for each claim
4. **Schema Validation**: Ensure all objects pass JSON validation
5. **HITL Review**: Human review of extracted objects
6. **Canonical Storage**: Store validated objects in src/ directory

## Evidence Chain Patterns
- **Source → Evidence**: Raw data becomes Evidence objects with PII
- **Evidence → Provenance**: Evidence molecules become Provenance objects
- **Provenance → DUX Objects**: Provenance supports Problem, Behavior, Result claims
- **DUX Objects → Insights**: Objects combine into synthesized insights

## Research Data Types
- **User Interviews**: Transcripts, notes, recordings
- **Surveys**: Quantitative and qualitative responses
- **Analytics**: Behavioral data, usage patterns
- **Observations**: Field studies, usability tests
- **Documents**: Reports, feedback, support tickets

## Evidence Maturity Tiers
1. **01_assumptive**: Inferred, no direct evidence
2. **02_anecdotal**: 1-2 qualitative signals
3. **03_early_signal**: Recurring signal, no quantitative data
4. **04_balanced_signal**: Qualitative + quantitative evidence
5. **05_triangulated**: Cross-method validation

## Provenance Object Structure
- **source_filename**: Original research file
- **timestamp_in/out**: Location within source
- **evidence_block**: Grouped evidence units
- **evidence_maturity**: System-derived quality tier
- **evidence_teaser**: Summary of key finding

## Insight Generation
- **Evidence Synthesis**: Combine multiple evidence sources
- **Pattern Recognition**: Identify recurring themes and behaviors
- **Hypothesis Formation**: Generate testable research questions
- **Impact Assessment**: Evaluate evidence strength and relevance

## Research Automation
- **Transcript Processing**: LLM extraction of DUX objects
- **Evidence Tagging**: Automatic categorization and maturity assessment
- **Insight Chaining**: fltr-based discovery of related insights
- **Quality Scoring**: Automated validation of evidence strength

## Data Privacy & Security
- **PII Handling**: Evidence objects contain PII, stay in research platform
- **Provenance Export**: Sanitized evidence molecules for external use
- **Access Control**: Role-based permissions for research data
- **Audit Trail**: Complete traceability of evidence usage

## Research Validation
- **Evidence Quality**: Assess source reliability and methodology
- **Sample Size**: Consider statistical significance and representativeness
- **Bias Detection**: Identify potential research biases and limitations
- **Triangulation**: Cross-validate findings across multiple sources
